Net Connect

A network puzzle game where you rotate tiles to connect all devices to the server. Plan your moves, solve the network, and earn stars!

Coming Soon on Google Play

Features

🧩

180 Campaign Levels

Three difficulty modes — Easy, Medium, and Hard — with 60 levels each. Progress through increasingly challenging network puzzles.

📅

Daily Puzzles

A fresh challenge every day. Come back daily for a new puzzle to solve and build your streak.

♾️

Endless Mode

Unlimited randomly generated puzzles across three difficulty levels. Play as much as you want, whenever you want.

☁️

Cloud Save

Sign in with Google to sync your progress across devices. Your stars and achievements travel with you.

🔍

Pinch to Zoom

Zoom into larger grids for precision. Single-finger pan when zoomed in to navigate the puzzle with ease.

💡

Hints & Undo

Stuck on a puzzle? Use hints to get unstuck. Made a wrong move? Undo it without penalty to keep your star rating.
