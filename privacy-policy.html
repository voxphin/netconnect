Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

Voxphin Apps ("we", "our", "us") operates the Net Connect mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

Google Account Information

When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture. This is used solely to identify your account for cloud save functionality.

Google Drive App Data

We store your game progress (campaign, daily puzzle, and endless mode data) in your Google Drive's private app data folder. Only Net Connect can access this data — no other apps or users can see it.

Game Data

We collect gameplay data such as level progress, star ratings, and settings preferences. This data is stored locally on your device and optionally synced to your Google Drive.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not collect device identifiers for advertising
  • We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties
  • We do not use analytics or tracking services
  • We do not sell personal information as defined by the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)

Data Storage and Security

Your game data is stored locally on your device and, if you opt in to cloud save, in your personal Google Drive app data folder. We do not maintain any external servers or databases that store your personal information.

Data Deletion

You can delete your cloud save data by revoking Net Connect's access in your Google Account settings (myaccount.google.com → Security → Third-party apps). You can also delete all local data by uninstalling the app.

Your Rights (EEA/GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at voxphin@gmail.com to exercise these rights.

Children's Privacy

Net Connect does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The game can be played without signing in.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at voxphin@gmail.com
