Data Deletion

Last updated: March 17, 2026

Net Connect by Voxphin Apps allows you to delete all data associated with your account. Here's how:

Delete Cloud Save Data

If you signed in with Google to use cloud save, your game progress is stored in your personal Google Drive app data folder. To delete it:

  1. Go to myaccount.google.com → Security
  2. Scroll to "Your connections to third-party apps & services"
  3. Find Net Connect and click on it
  4. Click "Delete all connections you have with Net Connect"
  5. This will revoke access and delete all app data stored in your Google Drive

Delete Local Data

Game data stored locally on your device (level progress, settings, star ratings) can be deleted by:

  1. Uninstalling the Net Connect app from your device, or
  2. Going to Settings → Apps → Net Connect → Clear Data

What Data Is Deleted

When you complete both steps above, the following data is permanently deleted:

  1. Google account info (name, email, profile picture) — removed when you revoke access
  2. Cloud save data (campaign progress, daily puzzle data, endless mode data) — removed from Google Drive
  3. Local game data (level progress, star ratings, settings) — removed from your device
Note: Voxphin Apps does not maintain any external servers or databases. All your data is stored either on your device or in your personal Google Drive. Once deleted, the data cannot be recovered.
